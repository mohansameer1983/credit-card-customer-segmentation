Key Questions¶

1.) How many different segments of customers are there?

  • There are appeared to be 3 segments, based on existing data:
  • Group 1 - 50 Customers - High Credit Limit, max no of cards, online visits maximum. Rarely make calls to bank and less visit to bank. No of customers are least in this group out of the 3 groups.
  • Group 2 - 223 Customers - Lowest Credit limit/no of credit cards. Less frequent visit to bank/less online visits. Mostly contact via phone.
  • Group 3 - 382 Customers - Average credit limit with comparatively high no of credit cards. Visit bank more frequently, and visit online rarely.

2.) How are these segments different from each other?

  • Customers with high avg credit limit are more using online services and have higher no of credit cards compared to other segments.
  • Customers with on an average less cards have lower credit limits. Mostly try to contact through phone.
  • 3rd type of customers are balanced one and have good no of credit cards, average credit limit. Either visits bank or make calls to bank. Rarely visit online.

3.) What are your recommendations to the bank on how to better market to and service these customers? Based on pattern we can suggest following:

  • Bank needs to improve online presence among group 2 and 3.
  • Bank can offer more through branch to group-3, when customers visits branch.
  • For group-1 customers, more digital ads can be targetted.
